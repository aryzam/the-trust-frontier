Methodology & transparency

Readiness scores synthesise published hardware roadmaps, peer-reviewed algorithm benchmarks, and expert literature as of May 2026. They are not original primary research — every score links to its source. Quantum computing and quantum sensing are categorised separately: sensing uses quantum phenomena to measure physical quantities with precision; computing uses quantum algorithms to process information. Most media conflates the two.

The author is a Princeton ECE student and IBM Qiskit Advocate. Scores reflect honest assessment including uncertainty. Corrections and challenges from domain experts are welcomed at thetrustfrontier.substack.com.
